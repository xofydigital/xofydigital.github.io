FYREVPN Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

FYREVPN provides VPN connectivity and related features. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app may process and for what purposes.

Data the app may process

  • Technical data required to establish and maintain the VPN connection.
  • User-selected app settings stored on the device.
  • Selected apps used for split tunneling features.
  • Diagnostic data related to server availability checks and connection status.

How data is used

  • To connect to VPN servers.
  • To apply user settings.
  • To support server selection and split tunneling.
  • To check server availability and improve connection stability.

Data storage

Some app settings may be stored locally on the user's device. This data is used only to operate the app and preserve selected preferences.

Sharing with third parties

The app may communicate with VPN servers and network infrastructure required to verify connectivity and provide VPN access. No other sharing with third parties is performed except where required by law or necessary for the technical operation of the service.

Security

The developer takes reasonable technical measures to protect data processed by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet can guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

The app is not specifically directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about FYREVPN, use the contact details provided on the app listing or developer profile.
